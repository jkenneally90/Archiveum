# Achiveum — Internal Company Profile & Office Navigation Guide

## Purpose of Document

This document provides a **comprehensive internal overview of Achiveum**, including employee roles, office layout, and navigation guidance. It is designed to support internal systems (including AI assistants) in answering location-based and personnel-related queries.

---

# 1. Company Overview

Achiveum is a private, experimental technology company focused on building **self-contained, privacy-first AI systems** that prioritize continuity, ownership, and personal intelligence.

Founded by:

* George (Director of Experience)
* Alice (Director of Cognitive Systems)
* James (Director of Engineering)

The company operates from a **three-floor office building**, structured to reflect workflow hierarchy and collaboration efficiency.

---

# 2. Building Overview

### Structure

* **Floor 3 (Top Floor): Executive & Strategy**
* **Floor 2 (Middle Floor): Marketing & Sales**
* **Floor 1 (Ground Floor): Development, Production & Design**

### General Navigation Rules

* Main entrance is on **Floor 1**
* Central staircase and lift are located in the **middle of each floor**
* Each floor is divided into **East Wing (right from stairs)** and **West Wing (left from stairs)**

# 4. Floor Layout Details & Directions

---

## FLOOR 1 — Development, Production & Design

### Layout

* **Reception desk directly ahead upon entry**
* Stairs/lift in the **center**
* West Wing: Engineering & Hardware
* East Wing: Design & Production

### Directions Example

* **To reach Lucas (1W-01):**

  * Enter building
  * Walk straight to stairs
  * Turn left (West Wing)
  * First office on the left

* **To reach Grace (Design Studio):**

  * Enter building
  * Turn right (East Wing)
  * Walk past glass wall studio
  * First desk inside design room
